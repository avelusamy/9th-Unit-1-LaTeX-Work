\section{}
    Let $A$ and $B$ be subsets of a set $Z$.
    Prove that $(Z\sm A)\cup(Z\sm B)=Z\sm(A\cap B)$.
    \begin{proof}
     Let $x \in (Z\sm A)\cup(Z\sm B)$. If $x\in Z\sm A$, then $x\in A$.
    \end{proof}
